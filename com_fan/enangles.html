List of possibilities:

Presencial

All the hours of the course will be held in one of the signed classrooms and must be attended in a presence in the building of the company in which it is located.

Telematic

The classes in this course will have to be done from the home computer and from home comfort. As the classes will be held in an assigned course advance time.

Semi-presential

In this case, there will be half the weekly hours that will have to be taken prisoner and the other half will have to be telematically.

Why do we do it?

  • Commodity

One of the reasons is for the best convenience of the study, in this way if any student should move without a necessary series, since they can take classes telematically.

  • Covid-19

The other reason for the telematic or semi-presential classes can be held is because of the epidemiological situation that currently exists for Covid-19.
